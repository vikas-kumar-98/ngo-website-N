Governance Leadership

Guiding HopeNexus with Excellence and Integrity

Our Board of Directors brings together distinguished leaders from diverse fields including business, philanthropy, international development, and academia.

The board provides strategic oversight, ensures financial accountability, and guides the organization toward achieving its mission. Each member serves voluntarily, bringing decades of collective experience to advance our cause.

Transparency

Board Meeting Schedule

Our board meets quarterly to review progress, approve budgets, and set strategic direction. All meetings are conducted in accordance with our bylaws and governance policies.

Quarter 1 (March) - Annual planning and budget approval
Quarter 2 (June) - Mid-year program review
Quarter 3 (September) - Financial audit review
Quarter 4 (December) - Annual impact assessment and strategic planning
