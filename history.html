A Legacy of Hope

From a Single Classroom to Global Impact

What began as a small tutoring program in New York City has blossomed into a worldwide movement for change.

In 2010, Dr. Sarah Chen started HopeNexus with just 5 volunteers and a dream to ensure every child has access to quality education. Today, we're proud to have impacted over 500,000 lives across 35+ countries with 150+ active projects.

Our journey is a testament to the power of collective action. Every milestone we've reached, every life we've touched, has been made possible by our dedicated volunteers, generous donors, and resilient partner communities.

Our Journey Through Time

Key Milestones in Our History

Every year brought new challenges, new opportunities, and new lives transformed

2010 The Beginning

Foundation Launched

Dr. Sarah Chen founded HopeNexus in New York City with 5 volunteers, starting an after-school tutoring program for 20 underprivileged children.

5 Volunteers | 20 Students | $500 Seed Fund
2012 Growing Reach

First School Partnership

Established partnerships with 10 local schools, expanding educational programs to reach 500 students. Introduced scholarship program for high-achieving students from low-income families.

10 Schools | 500 Students | 50 Scholarships
2014 Global Expansion

International Expansion

Launched first international programs in Kenya and India, bringing education and healthcare to rural communities. Opened regional offices in Nairobi and Mumbai.

2 Countries | 2 Regional Offices | 50+ International Volunteers
2016 Women's Empowerment

Women's Empowerment Initiative

Launched comprehensive women's empowerment programs including vocational training, microfinance, and leadership development across 5 countries.

5 Countries | 1,000+ Women Trained | $500K Microfinance Fund
2018 Healthcare Launch

Healthcare Initiative Launched

Introduced mobile health clinics providing free medical care, vaccinations, and health education to 50+ remote villages across Africa and Asia.

15 Mobile Clinics | 100,000+ Patients | 50K+ Vaccinations
2020 COVID Response

Emergency COVID-19 Response

Mobilized rapid-response teams, providing food, medical supplies, and financial assistance to over 50,000 families affected by the pandemic globally.

50K+ Families | 1M+ Meals | $2M+ Emergency Aid
2022 Climate Action

Climate Action Program

Launched global environmental initiative, planting over 1 million trees and implementing sustainable farming practices across 15 countries.

1M+ Trees Planted | 15 Countries | 50+ Sustainable Projects
2024 Major Milestone

500,000 Lives Milestone

Celebrated impacting over 500,000 lives across 35+ countries with 150+ active projects and 25,000+ dedicated volunteers worldwide.

500K+ Lives | 35+ Countries | 25K+ Volunteers

Founder's Reflection

A Message from Our Founder

"When I started HopeNexus in my small apartment with just a handful of volunteers, I never imagined we would grow into a global movement. But I've learned that hope is contagious. When people see that change is possible, they want to be part of it. Today, as I look back at our journey, I'm filled with gratitude for every volunteer, every donor, and every community member who believed in this dream. Our history is still being written, and the best chapters are yet to come."

— Dr. Sarah Chen

Founder & CEO, HopeNexus Foundation
