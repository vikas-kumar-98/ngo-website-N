Our Policies

Financial Policies & Controls

HopeNexus Foundation maintains rigorous financial policies to ensure responsible stewardship of donor funds.

  • Annual independent financial audit
  • Internal control systems and procedures
  • Whistleblower protection policy
  • Conflict of interest policy
  • Investment policy and oversight
  • Expense reimbursement policy

Financial Oversight

The Finance Committee of the Board of Directors provides oversight of all financial matters, including review of audited statements and internal controls.

Committee Chair: Robert Williams, CPA

Meets quarterly to review financial performance
